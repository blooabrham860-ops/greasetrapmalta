Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should grease trap cleaning be done?

Most commercial kitchens need grease trap cleaning every 1–3 months, depending on usage and local regulations.

2. What happens if a grease trap is not cleaned regularly?

Neglected traps can cause blockages, odors, plumbing damage, and health code violations.

3. Is grease trap cleaning required by law?

Yes, many states and cities in the United States require documented grease trap maintenance.

4. How long does a grease trap clean up take?

Typically, grease trap clean up takes 30 minutes to a few hours, depending on system size.

5. What is included in grease trap waste removal?

It includes removing grease, solids, wastewater, and transporting waste for proper processing.
